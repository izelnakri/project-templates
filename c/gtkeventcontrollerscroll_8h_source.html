41/**
42 * GtkEventControllerScrollFlags:
43 * @GTK_EVENT_CONTROLLER_SCROLL_NONE: Don't emit scroll.
44 * @GTK_EVENT_CONTROLLER_SCROLL_VERTICAL: Emit scroll with vertical deltas.
45 * @GTK_EVENT_CONTROLLER_SCROLL_HORIZONTAL: Emit scroll with horizontal deltas.
46 * @GTK_EVENT_CONTROLLER_SCROLL_DISCRETE: Only emit deltas that are multiples of 1.
47 * @GTK_EVENT_CONTROLLER_SCROLL_KINETIC: Emit ::decelerate after continuous scroll finishes.
48 * @GTK_EVENT_CONTROLLER_SCROLL_BOTH_AXES: Emit scroll on both axes.
49 *
