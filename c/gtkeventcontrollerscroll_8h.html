◆ GtkEventControllerScrollFlags

enum GtkEventControllerScrollFlags

GtkEventControllerScrollFlags: @GTK_EVENT_CONTROLLER_SCROLL_NONE: Don't emit scroll. @GTK_EVENT_CONTROLLER_SCROLL_VERTICAL: Emit scroll with vertical deltas. @GTK_EVENT_CONTROLLER_SCROLL_HORIZONTAL: Emit scroll with horizontal deltas. @GTK_EVENT_CONTROLLER_SCROLL_DISCRETE: Only emit deltas that are multiples of 1. @GTK_EVENT_CONTROLLER_SCROLL_KINETIC: Emit ::decelerate after continuous scroll finishes. @GTK_EVENT_CONTROLLER_SCROLL_BOTH_AXES: Emit scroll on both axes.

Describes the behavior of a GtkEventControllerScroll.
